Ethical Dilemma Response

During a group design project, one team member suggested manipulating test data to make results appear more favorable. As the team leader, I held a meeting to discuss the importance of integrity in engineering. We decided to report accurate results and improve our design instead. My decision was based on long-term credibility, respect for public safety, and alignment with professional standards.
