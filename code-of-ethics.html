Engineering Leadership Code of Ethics

  • Integrity: Always report data honestly and avoid manipulation.
  • Transparency: Communicate decisions clearly to stakeholders.
  • Safety First: Prioritize safety over deadlines or cost-cutting.
  • Inclusivity: Respect diversity and ensure fair team collaboration.
  • Accountability: Take ownership of both success and failure.
  • Continuous Learning: Keep improving skills and ethical knowledge.
