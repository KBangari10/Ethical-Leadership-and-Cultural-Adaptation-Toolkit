Performance Review Coaching Script

I appreciate your hard work this sprint. However, we noticed some delays and extra reviews were required. I’d like to understand if workload, task complexity, or time constraints are affecting your output. We’re here to support you—whether that means breaking down tasks better or assigning a mentor. Let’s work together to set new, realistic goals for the next sprint. I believe in your potential and want to see you succeed.
